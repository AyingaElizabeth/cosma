About the Program

Education + Livelihoods for Lasting Change

This is the same Education Program described on the site: a child sponsorship programme designed around COSMA’s Enterprise-to-Education model. Sponsorship keeps children in school while household income support creates the conditions for long-term sustainability.

The programme supports school fees, learning materials, mentorship, and wellbeing, while also helping families build income through enterprise activities and savings groups.

Protect Education

Cover school costs, materials, and mentoring support.

Strengthen Incomes

Help households access practical livelihood opportunities.

Build Savings

Households join savings groups and improve financial planning.

Transition to Self-Reliance

Families gradually take on more school costs as incomes grow.

Programme Impact

Education with a Path to Independence

Through this programme, we are shifting from one-off sponsorship to an integrated household solution. Children stay in school while families build the skills and savings needed to support their education long-term.

Your sponsorship becomes part of a holistic system that links income generation, education support, and financial resilience—so families can thrive beyond the sponsorship period.
