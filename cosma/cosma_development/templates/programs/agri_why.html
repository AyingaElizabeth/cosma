Agriculture Program

Why Agriculture?

Over 70% of Ugandans depend on agriculture. It is the engine of food security, income, and community resilience.

The Challenge

Feeding Families, Building Futures

Smallholder farmers in Uganda face low yields, unpredictable weather, limited market access, and lack of affordable inputs. These challenges trap families in cycles of poverty.

HopeForward's agriculture programme equips farmers with modern techniques, climate-smart practices, and market connections to break that cycle permanently.
