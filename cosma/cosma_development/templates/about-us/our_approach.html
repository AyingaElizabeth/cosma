Our Philosophy

Community-Driven Development

Our work is guided by a community-driven development approach that places communities at the centre of their own transformation. We believe lasting change happens when communities are organised, empowered, and equipped to lead their own development.

Rather than providing short-term support, we build and strengthen local systems — enabling communities to take collective action, manage resources effectively, and sustain long-term progress.

Our Approach Models

Two Integrated Delivery Models, One Holistic Approach to Community Transformation COSMA’s Approach

We place communities at the center of their own transformation. We organize, empower, and equip communities to lead their own development and sustain lasting change.

VILLAGE COOPERATIVE MODEL

(AGRICULTURE)
Organizing farmers into structured farmers’ groups (Village Cooperatives)
Agronomic training
Improved agricultural production
Building financial literacy and access to financing
Strengthening collective market participation

ENTERPRISE-TO-EDUCATION MODEL

(EDUCATION)
Mobilizing vulnerable households, particularly the land constrained ones
Engaging in income-generating activities
Generating income and strengthening livelihoods
(Co-)financing and sustaining children’s education

Our Interventions

How We Deliver Our Mission

We advance our mission through integrated, community-driven interventions. By linking livelihoods to education, we empower households to not only improve their incomes — but to sustain long-term progress for their children and communities.
