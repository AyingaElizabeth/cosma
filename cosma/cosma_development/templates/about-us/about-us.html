Our Story

From a Small Idea to National Impact

cosma developments was founded in 2006 by a group of Ugandan educators and community workers who saw firsthand the barriers preventing families from thriving — lack of access to quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods.

What started as a small after-school programme in Kampala has grown into a national organisation working across 12 districts, serving over 125,000 individuals annually.
