Who We Are

Building Self-Reliant Communities

COSMA Sustainable Rural Development is a Ugandan NGO committed to building prosperous and self-reliant rural communities.

We deliver integrated agriculture and education programs through a community-driven approach that strengthens incomes and expands opportunities for vulnerable households.

Since our establishment, we have reached thousands of farmers and supported children to stay in school.

Our Impact
  • 1,750+ farmers directly supported
  • 7,000+ individuals impacted
  • 120+ children supported in school
